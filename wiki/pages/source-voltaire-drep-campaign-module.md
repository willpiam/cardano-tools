## Summary
This source is a web presentation of CIP-1694 content aimed at explaining Voltaire governance and DRep participation. It restates the governance model, action lifecycle, and ratification structure while adding campaign-oriented framing (interactive explainer style, workshop/changelog context, and practical participation messaging).

## Key claims
- Governance follows a five-stage lifecycle: submission, deliberation, voting, ratification, and enactment.
- Any Ada holder can submit governance actions by posting a deposit, and voting is organized across constitutional committee, DReps, and SPOs depending on action type.
- DRep participation is stake-weighted with explicit predefined delegation options (`Abstain` and `No Confidence`).
- An optional guardrails script can constrain treasury withdrawals and protocol parameter changes.
- Bootstrapping is phased: initial restrictions apply before full DRep-centered governance activation.

## Notable details captured in this source format
